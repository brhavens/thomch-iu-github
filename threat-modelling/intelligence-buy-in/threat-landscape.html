--- layout: iu-template title: "Threat Landscape" ---
  • Financial/Payment Frauds
    • Affecting online businesses from the start
    • Hackers can make unapproved transactions and potentially delete the financial trace
      • Can cause substantial loss
    • More complex and are difficult to distinguish with rules-based systems today
      • Differentiation of digital footprint, structure
  • Bots
    • Exclusively developed to extract website pricing and inventory information
    • Information is used by hackers to negatively effect sales and revenue of businesses
  • DDOS/DOS Attacks
    • Negatively affect overall sales
      • Flood servers
      • Disrupt website
