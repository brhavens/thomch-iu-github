  • Network infrastructure is the hardware and software resources of an enterprise network that enable network connectivity, communication, processing and storing of data. It provides a communication path between users, processes, applications, servers, and storage devices.​
    • Network Infrastructure are the routers, switches, computers, and servers (and their operating systems) that process and transmit data. It also includes storage systems and networks​​
