The Role of Information Technology Cannot be Overstated

  • Telecommunication networks must exist to physically connect the industry players together to process electronic payments
  • Websites, applications, databases are necessary to support digital payments
  • IT security must protect the financial assets of customers and business both in transit and at rest.
  • The industry simply could not exist without IT to transmit, process, store, and secure transactions
